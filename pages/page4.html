Jeff Bezos

Early Life, Education and Career

Jeffrey Preston Jorgensen was born on January 12, 1964 in Albuquerque, New Mexico. His parents divorced and his mother married Cuban immigrant, Mike Bezos. He was later adopted by Bezos and that is how his last name changed. He attended high school in Florida and was valedictorian. In 1986, he graduated from Princeton University with a BS in electrical engineering and computer science.

After graduating from University, he first worked at Fitel, a fintech telecommunications start-up company, where he built a network for international trade. He then transition ed into the banking industry when he became a product manager at Bankers Trust. Next, he moved to D.E. Shaw and Co., a newly founded hedge fund with a strong emphasis on mathematical modeling.

Amazon

In late 1993, Bezos decided to leave his job at D.E. Shaw and establish an online bookstore. He founded Amazon in his garage on July 5, 1994 after writing its business plan on a roadtrip from NYC to Seattle. The companu was initially named Cadabra, but was later changed to Amazon. He took around $300k from his parents and invested it into Amazon. In 1998, he diversified into the online sale of music and video, and by the end of the year he expanded the company's products to include a variety of consumer goods. In 2002, he launched Amazon Web Services, which compiled data from weather chammels and website traffic.
