Elon Musk

Early Life & Education

Elon Reeve Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa on June 28, 1971. He was born and raised in Pretoria, South Africa, and moved to Canada when he was 17 years old to attend Queen's University. After 2 years, he transferred to UPenn and received a bachelor's degree in economics from Wharton and a abchelor's degree in physics from the College of Arts and Sciences. He started his Ph.D. in applied physics at Stanford University in 1995, but quite 2 days later to pursue a business career.
